# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in AegisOps AI a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Public or private sexual harassment
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers at **shreyashtapke@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly.
